🌿 Amazonia Pai D’égua Store

It all started when a group of UEPA students tried to make tacacá in the chemistry lab. The jambu escaped the pot, the tucupi boiled over, and someone shouted: “This is way too pai d’égua!” That’s when the idea was born: create a store with regional kits so no one has to become a chemist to taste the Amazon.
